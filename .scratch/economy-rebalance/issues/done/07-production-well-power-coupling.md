# Production wells consume power + throttle on undersupply (15 kWh/bbl)

Status: ready-for-agent
Category: enhancement

## Parent

[PRD: Economy & balance pass](../PRD.md)

## What to build

Couple production wells to the grid the same way injection wells already are: a production well consumes electricity proportional to throughput, and its actual output throttles when the grid cannot meet its power demand. This restores symmetry between production and injection (which costs 50 kWh/bbl) and ensures an undersupplied grid degrades oil production proportionally rather than ignoring it.

Implementation:

- New constant `PRODUCTION_KWH_PER_BBL = 15.0` alongside `INJECTION_KWH_PER_BBL = 50.0`.
- A symmetric production-well counterpart to the existing injection-well power-cost helper in `world/pricing.py`, returning `current_rate × PRODUCTION_KWH_PER_BBL`.
- Sim-time throttling block in `world/sim.py` that mirrors injection-well throttling: each hour, a production well's actual rate is capped by `power_kw / PRODUCTION_KWH_PER_BBL`.

## Agent Brief

**Category:** enhancement
**Summary:** Couple production wells to the grid at 15 kWh/bbl — power-cost the throughput, and throttle output when the grid cannot supply.

**Current behavior:**
Injection wells consume electricity at 50 kWh/bbl and throttle when the grid is under-supplied. Production wells consume no electricity, so oil production is decoupled from power decisions and an under-supplied grid has no effect on oil output.

**Desired behavior:**
Production wells consume 15 kWh/bbl symmetrically to injection. At full power supply, a production well runs at its setpoint. When the grid cannot meet a production well's demand, its actual hourly output is capped at `power_kw / 15.0` (mirroring the existing injection-well throttling). A symmetric pricing helper exists for production-well power cost (`current_rate × 15.0`).

**Key interfaces:**
- A new module-level constant `PRODUCTION_KWH_PER_BBL = 15.0` consumed by both pricing and sim.
- A new pricing helper that mirrors the injection-well power-cost helper, returning `current_rate × PRODUCTION_KWH_PER_BBL` for a given rate.
- The hourly sim loop's production-well step — applies the same throttling pattern the injection-well step uses, using `PRODUCTION_KWH_PER_BBL` as the divisor.
- Injection-well behavior — untouched. `INJECTION_KWH_PER_BBL = 50.0` remains.

**Acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] `PRODUCTION_KWH_PER_BBL` equals 15.0 and is used by both pricing and sim.
- [ ] The pricing helper returns `current_rate × 15.0` for any given rate.
- [ ] At full power supply, a production well runs at its setpoint.
- [ ] Under-supplied, hourly throughput equals `power_kw / 15.0`.
- [ ] Injection-well behavior (50 kWh/bbl path) is unchanged.
- [ ] `make check` passes.

**Out of scope:**
- Changing injection-well kWh/bbl or its throttling behavior.
- Reordering well dispatch priority versus other loads.
- Per-well or per-tile electricity metering UI.
