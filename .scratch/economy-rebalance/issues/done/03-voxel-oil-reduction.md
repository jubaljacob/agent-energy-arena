# Per-voxel oil-in-place reduced 20% (new worlds only)

Status: ready-for-agent
Category: enhancement

## Parent

[PRD: Economy & balance pass](../PRD.md)

## What to build

Drop `VOXEL_VOLUME_BBL` in the subsurface module from 70,000 to 56,000 so that reservoirs hold roughly 20% less oil per voxel and depletion becomes a credible mid-to-late-game pressure within a typical play horizon. Reservoir geometry (blob count, radius, density / `HC_PROBABILITY_BASE`) is unchanged — only per-voxel volumetrics shrink. Existing saved scenarios (e.g. `scenarios/longest_3789_days/`) continue to load with their original per-voxel values; the new constant applies only to newly generated worlds.

- [ ] Reservoir geometry (blob count, radius range, density) is unchanged.

## Agent Brief

**Category:** enhancement
**Summary:** Shrink per-voxel oil-in-place by 20% so depletion becomes credible within one play session, without disturbing existing saved scenarios.

**Current behavior:**
Reservoirs hold so much oil per voxel that a typical play session cannot deplete them. Subsurface strategy collapses to "drill once, run forever". The current per-voxel volume is 70,000 bbl.

**Desired behavior:**
Per-voxel oil-in-place drops to 56,000 bbl (a 20% reduction) for **newly generated worlds**. Reservoir geometry (blob count, radius range, base hydrocarbon probability/density) is unchanged. Existing saved/recorded scenarios continue to reproduce their original economics — the new constant must not retroactively alter scenarios loaded from disk.

**Key interfaces:**
- The `VOXEL_VOLUME_BBL` constant in the subsurface module — value becomes 56000.
- World-generation entry points that consume this constant — they continue to use the constant directly.
- Saved-scenario loading — preserves whatever per-voxel value was stored at save time. (If volumes are derived at runtime rather than persisted, a fixture-based test must demonstrate that a recorded scenario reproduces its original depletion curve after the constant change.)

**Acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] `VOXEL_VOLUME_BBL` equals 56000.
- [ ] A freshly generated world reports per-voxel volume of 56000 bbl.
- [ ] A loaded fixture scenario (e.g. `scenarios/longest_3789_days/` or equivalent) reproduces its original per-voxel oil and unchanged depletion trajectory.
- [ ] Blob count, radius range, and `HC_PROBABILITY_BASE` are unchanged.
- [ ] `make check` passes.

**Out of scope:**
- Reservoir geometry (blob count, radius, density).
- Exploration mechanics, well placement heuristics, drilling cost changes.
- Retroactive migration of saved scenarios to the new constant.
